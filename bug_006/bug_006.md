# Bug 006 — RETRACTED

**This report was based on incorrect test analysis and does not describe a real bug.**

## What went wrong in the analysis

The initial report claimed that TC links were incorrectly attributed to Version 1 rather than Version 2 in the rich-text XLS export. This was caused by analyzing the wrong file — `comprehensive-export.xls` was analyzed instead of `version2-export.xls`. The comprehensive export was made *before* Version 2 of SC-001 was created; at that point, the TC link correctly belonged to Version 1 (the only version). After Version 2 was created, the TC link migrated to Version 2 — and the later export (`version2-export.xls`) correctly reflects this.

## Confirmed correct behavior

With "Keep the rich text format" enabled, the export after Version 2 creation shows:

**REQUIREMENT sheet:**

| REQ_VERSION_NUM | REQ_VERSION_ID | REQ_VERSION_#_TC |
|-----------------|----------------|------------------|
| 1 | 5 | 0 |
| 2 | 14 | 1 |

**LINK_REQ_TC sheet:**

| REQ_PATH | REQ_VERSION_NUM | TC_PATH |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| /TestProject/Special-Chars Test/REQ-SC01 … | 2 | /TestProject/TC-REQ-SC01 |

TC links are correctly attributed to Version 2. No bug exists here.
